Purple Ruler Academy - Mentor Management System

A comprehensive Wix-based platform for mentor dashboard, student management, session scheduling, finance tracking, and reporting

Welcome to Your Mentor Management System

Purple Ruler Academy Mentor Management System is designed to simplify your daily teaching and administrative tasks. This comprehensive platform helps you manage your students, schedule sessions, track finances, and generate reports - all in one easy-to-use interface.

Key Features

Mentor Dashboard

Comprehensive overview with statistics, quick actions, data visualization, and responsive design for mentor workflow management.

Student Management

Complete student CRUD operations, search & filtering, bulk operations, and communication features with email integration.

Session Management

Advanced session scheduling, status management, notification system, and calendar view with Wix CMS integration.

Finance Management

Fee calculation, payment tracking, financial reporting, and income statistics with automated processing.

Reports System

Data analysis, report generation, export functionality, and visualization charts for comprehensive insights.

Email Service

Integrated email service with notification, confirmation, reminder, and general message types for student communication.

How to Use Your System

  • Dashboard Overview: Get a quick snapshot of your students, upcoming sessions, and financial summary
  • Easy Navigation: Use the sidebar menu to access different sections of your management system
  • Mobile Friendly: Access your system from any device - desktop, tablet, or smartphone
  • Automated Notifications: Receive and send email updates to keep everyone informed
  • Secure Data: Your student and financial information is safely stored and protected
  • Real-time Updates: Changes are saved automatically and reflected immediately

Getting Started Guide

  1. Dashboard: Start here to see your daily overview and quick actions
  2. Add Students: Go to Student Management to register new students and manage their profiles
  3. Schedule Sessions: Use Session Management to book lessons and track attendance
  4. Track Payments: Monitor fees and payments in the Finance section
  5. Generate Reports: Create detailed reports for student progress and business insights
  6. Send Messages: Communicate with students and parents through the integrated email system

System Benefits

  • Save Time: Automate routine tasks like scheduling and payment tracking
  • Stay Organized: Keep all student information and session details in one place
  • Improve Communication: Send professional emails and updates to students and parents
  • Track Progress: Monitor student development and business performance
  • Reduce Paperwork: Digital records eliminate the need for manual filing
  • Access Anywhere: Manage your teaching business from any location

Support & Help

Need assistance? Each section includes helpful guides and tooltips. The system is designed to be intuitive, but if you need help getting started or have questions about any features, support documentation is available within each module.
