How do I request an extension for my assignment?
To request an extension, you need to contact your teacher directly through the messaging system or email at least 48 hours before the deadline. Provide a valid reason for your request and any supporting documentation if applicable.
I can't access my course materials. What should I do?
First, try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then restart your browser. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. If you still can't access your materials, submit a technical support ticket with details about the specific course and materials you're trying to access.
How can I access counseling services?
You can access counseling services by visiting the Wellbeing section of your portal, clicking on "Talk to Someone" and booking an appointment. Alternatively, you can speak with your form tutor or head of year who can make a referral on your behalf. All counseling services are confidential.
How do I update my personal information?
You can update your personal information by going to your profile settings. Click on your profile picture in the sidebar, select "Settings," and then "Personal Information." Make the necessary changes and click "Save." For certain information like legal name changes, you may need to contact the administration office.
Where can I find past exam papers?
Past exam papers can be found in the Resources section of each course. Navigate to your course page, click on "Resources," and then "Past Assessments." If you can't find what you're looking for, ask your subject teacher who can provide additional resources.
