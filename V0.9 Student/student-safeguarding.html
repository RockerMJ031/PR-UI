SAFE Guidelines

Remember these important principles for your safety and wellbeing

S

Speak up

Your voice matters. Don't hesitate to report concerns or ask questions about your safety.

A

Ask for help

Reach out to trusted adults, teachers, or support services when you need assistance.

F

Feelings matter

Your emotions and wellbeing are important. It's okay to express how you feel.

E

Everyone needs support

You're not alone. Support is available and asking for help shows strength.

What is Safeguarding?

  • Protecting you from harm and abuse
  • Ensuring your physical and emotional wellbeing
  • Creating a safe learning environment
  • Supporting your development and growth
  • Promoting your welfare and happiness

When to Report

  • If you feel unsafe or threatened
  • Physical, emotional, or sexual abuse
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Neglect or inappropriate care
  • Online safety concerns
  • Substance abuse issues
  • Mental health concerns

Your Rights

  • To be heard and taken seriously
  • To receive appropriate support
  • To have your privacy respected
  • To be protected from harm
  • To access confidential help
  • To be treated with dignity and respect
