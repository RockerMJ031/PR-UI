Student Safeguarding

Your safety and wellbeing are our top priority. Here you'll find information about safeguarding, how to report concerns, and where to get help.

What is Safeguarding?

Protection

  • Protecting you from abuse, neglect, and harm
  • Ensuring your physical and emotional safety
  • Supporting your wellbeing and development
  • Preventing exploitation and discrimination
  • Promoting your rights and dignity

When to Report

  • Physical, emotional, or sexual abuse
  • Neglect or abandonment
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Discrimination or hate incidents
  • Online safety concerns
  • Substance abuse issues
  • Mental health crises
  • Domestic violence

Your Rights

  • To be safe and protected from harm
  • To be listened to and taken seriously
  • To receive appropriate support
  • To confidentiality and privacy
  • To be involved in decisions about you
  • To access independent advocacy
  • To make complaints if needed

Report a Concern

Choose the most appropriate way to report your concern. All reports are taken seriously and handled confidentially.

Safeguarding Form

Report serious safeguarding concerns including abuse, neglect, or immediate safety risks. This form goes directly to our safeguarding team.

Bullying Report

Report bullying, harassment, or discrimination. We take all forms of bullying seriously and will investigate promptly.

Email Contact

Send a confidential email to our safeguarding team. You can include as much or as little detail as you feel comfortable sharing.
